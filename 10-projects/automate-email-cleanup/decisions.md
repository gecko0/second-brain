## 2026-05-16 - Track This As A Project

Decision:
Evaluate automated email cleanup as an active project.

Reason:
The work has a concrete outcome: choose or build a system that categorizes email, removes low-value messages from the inbox, and supports AI automations.

Tradeoffs:
Off-the-shelf email clients may be easier to start with. A custom Codex plus Google Workspace CLI workflow may offer more control and explainability but requires more setup and guardrails.
