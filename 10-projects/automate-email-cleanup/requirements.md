# Requirements

## Must-Have

- Reduce inbox noise from spam, cold outreach, newsletters, and low-value notifications.
- Categorize emails into useful groups.
- Avoid deleting important emails.
- Make automation decisions explainable and reviewable.
- Support Gmail or Google Workspace.
- Allow human review for risky actions.

## Nice-To-Have

- AI-based categorization.
- Automatic unsubscribe suggestions.
- Automatic archive or delete for obvious low-value email.
- Daily digest of what changed.
- Labels for work, personal, finance, travel, receipts, newsletters, cold outreach, and action-required.
- Custom rules that can evolve over time.
- Integration with Codex or scheduled AI tasks.
- CLI-based workflow for version-controlled automation.
