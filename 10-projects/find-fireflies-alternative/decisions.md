# Decisions

## 2026-05-16 - Track This As A Project

Decision:
Evaluate Fireflies alternatives as an active project.

Reason:
The work has a concrete outcome: choose whether to stay with Fireflies, move to another SaaS tool, or build a local/self-hosted workflow.

Tradeoffs:
Cloud SaaS options are likely easier to use. Local and self-hosted options may provide more control and privacy but require more setup and maintenance.

## 2026-05-16 - Prioritize Price And Recording Capacity

Decision:
Make price and high-volume meeting recording the primary evaluation criteria.

Reason:
Fireflies is expensive, and the meeting volume is high. The system does not need to transcribe every meeting, but it does need to record meetings reliably.

Tradeoffs:
The best workflow may separate recording from transcription. This could reduce cost, but it may require more manual steps or automation.
