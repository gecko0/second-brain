## Test Method

Use the same sample meeting recording for each realistic candidate.

Record:

- Transcription quality
- Ukrainian transcription quality
- Risk of confusing Ukrainian with Polish
- Recording reliability
- Recording limits
- Cost per recorded meeting
- Cost per transcribed meeting
- Speaker label quality
- Summary usefulness
- Action item quality
- Time to process
- Setup effort
- Export quality
- Searchability
- Privacy concerns
- Total cost
