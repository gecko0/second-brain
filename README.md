# Second Brain

This is a Markdown-based second brain designed to be opened as an Obsidian vault and maintained by both humans and AI agents.

## Structure

- `00-inbox/` - Quick capture for unsorted thoughts, links, meeting notes, and raw material.
- `10-projects/` - Active work with a clear outcome or finish line.
- `20-areas/` - Ongoing responsibilities and standards to maintain.
- `30-resources/` - Topic-based reference material.
- `40-notes/` - Evergreen notes: durable ideas written in your own words.
- `50-archive/` - Completed, inactive, or superseded material.
- `90-meta/` - Templates, indexes, workflows, and vault maintenance notes.

## Operating Rules

1. Capture first in `00-inbox/` when the destination is unclear.
2. Move active outcomes into `10-projects/`.
3. Keep ongoing responsibilities in `20-areas/`.
4. Store reusable references in `30-resources/`.
5. Distill reusable ideas into `40-notes/`.
6. Archive instead of deleting when something may have future context value.
7. Prefer small, linked Markdown files over large documents.
8. Use relative Markdown links so notes work in Obsidian and plain text tools.
