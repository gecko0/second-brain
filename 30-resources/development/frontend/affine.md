# AFFiNE

Sources:

- https://github.com/toeverything/AFFiNE
- https://affine.pro/

Captured: 2026-06-29

AFFiNE is an open-source, local-first knowledge workspace positioned as an alternative to Notion and Miro. It combines docs, canvas/whiteboard, tables, planning, and knowledge management in one product.

Use this mainly as a source-code reference for building:

- Mind maps and visual knowledge structures
- Content editors and block-based document editing
- Infinite boards/canvases where people can upload, arrange, and work with content
- Multi-user collaboration patterns
- Local-first workspace UX and data flow

Notes:

- Repository owner: `toeverything`
- Product site: AFFiNE Pro
- The repo includes `blocksuite`, `packages`, `docs`, `tests`, and tooling that may be useful to study separately.
- The README describes AFFiNE as privacy-focused, local-first, open-source, and ready to use.
- Treat it as a code reference/template for projects that need collaborative editing, document/canvas hybrids, or digital asset workspaces.
