# AI Product Design Patterns

## Summary

Reference sources for designing AI-native product experiences and learning from existing AI UX patterns.

## Key Points

- [AIverse](https://www.aiverse.design/) is a playbook for designing AI-native products, aimed at product and design teams building AI features.
- It includes 300+ curated AI UX references and an AI patterns framework across onboarding, input, output, refinement, and learning.
- Useful pattern areas include knowledge bases and integrations, processing steps, streaming output, citations, explainability, and AI product adaptation over time.
- The site also offers insights, design tools, a glossary, an AI Design Studio, and a newsletter for ongoing AI UX examples.
- Treat it as a design reference and inspiration source rather than implementation documentation.
